Medispenser

An automated pill dispenser for clinical and assisted care environments. An RFID reader looks up the patient's prescription, and three slotted-disk hoppers driven by steppers dispense the correct combination and dosage. Achieved 100% accuracy across 100 trials at a ~$70 build cost.

Engineering Disaster Game

A three-stage interactive exhibit teaching 4th–5th graders about the Hyatt Regency Walkway Collapse. Combines a Python narrative game, a laser-cut bridge puzzle, and a Raspberry Pi Pico–driven 3D bridge that physically collapses when overloaded — with LCD readout and an LED warning strip.
